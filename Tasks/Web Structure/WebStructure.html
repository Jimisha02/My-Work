What we eat is what makes our body and mind. Every cell in the body and every thought in the mind is made by the food from mother nature. If we have polluted everything from water, air, soil, seeds, leaves, vegetables and fruits then our body and mind will also be polluted. So we decided to grow 100 % organic produce in the ashram 2 years ago. We neither wanted to use pesticide nor fertilizer nor GMO seeds. Instead we acquired traditionally preserved seeds, ashram cow dung, cow urine, and some other natural leaves of trees in the ashram. And we are very happy to inform that we have made it possible and are now growing more than 15 varieties of fruits, 4 types of grains, 3 legumes, 3 nuts, 4 beans and 12 different vegetables. All of this produce has the special ashram energy and is inviting you all back to the ashram again.
